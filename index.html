Overview

Referred to as the cultural capital of Karnataka, Mysore, officially Mysuru, is a city in the southern part of the state of Karnataka, India. Mysore is famous not only for its palaces but also for Dasara festival, Mysore silk saris, sandalwood and the sweet named Mysore pak. It is the erstwhile capital of the Mysore Maharajas, who ruled the Mysore State between 1399 & 1947. Mysore still retains its old world charm with its palaces, heritage buildings, traditions and temples. At an altitude of 763 meters surrounded by hill ranges from north to south, it is known as the City of Palaces. Mysore is one of the top destinations to visit, no matter where you are from The various sites and palaces in this beautiful city give the words “A sight to behold” a whole new meaning.
